Privacy Policy for The Minimalist Flow Watch Face

Effective date: May 13, 2026

The Minimalist Flow Watch Face is a Wear OS watch face published by Flowing Soft. This policy explains how the watch face handles information.

Information We Collect

The Minimalist Flow Watch Face does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal information to Flowing Soft or to third parties.

Watch Face Data

The watch face may display information provided by Wear OS complications, such as calendar or task-related text, when you choose to configure those complications on your device. That information is handled locally by Wear OS and the complication provider. The Minimalist Flow Watch Face does not send that information to Flowing Soft.

Permissions

The Minimalist Flow Watch Face is a resource-only watch face and does not request device permissions for collecting personal data.

Children's Privacy

The watch face is designed to be calm and readable for family routines, including use by children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

The Minimalist Flow Watch Face does not include analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

Flowing Soft may update this policy when the watch face changes. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact Flowing Soft at flowingsoft@flowingsoft.com.
